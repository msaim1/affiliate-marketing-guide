# Affiliate Marketing Guide 🔁

> A complete guide to building and scaling profitable affiliate marketing campaigns.

---

## 💡 What is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a performance-based model where you earn commission
by promoting other people's products. You only pay when a sale is made.

---

## 🏗️ Affiliate Marketing Models

| Model | How It Works |
|-------|-------------|
| **CPS** | Commission per sale (most common) |
| **CPL** | Commission per lead generated |
| **CPC** | Commission per click |
| **Revenue Share** | % of ongoing customer revenue |

## 💰 Scaling Your Affiliate Income

### Month 1–2: Foundation
- Pick 1 niche, 1 traffic source
- Test 3–5 different offers
- Create 20+ pieces of content

### Month 3–4: Optimize
- Double down on best performing content
- Build email list for retargeting
- Add paid ads to winning organic content

### Month 5–6: Scale
- Expand to new traffic sources
- Negotiate higher commission rates
- Build a team or outsource content
